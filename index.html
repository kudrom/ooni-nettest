Only the map and the histogram can be interacted with

If you want to select the reports made in China, you should left click in China and see how the other visualizations change.

If you want to remove the reports made in China, you should Ctrl + left click in China and see how the other visualizations change.

If you want to remove the reports made in China and India, you should Ctrl + left click in China and Ctrl + Shift + left click in India.

You can do the same previous three actions with any country in the map and any nettest in the histogram.

You should click the Reset button in the top right corner when you want to do another selection or removal, don't stack the selections.
